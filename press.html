FREE iOS PUZZLE GAME TRANSFORMS SQUARE INSTAGRAM PHOTOS INTO FIENDISHLY SCRAMBLED CIRCLES

San Francisco, CA — May 31, 2012 — Amphistyle, Inc., is delighted to announce the launch of CircleGram, a free puzzle game for iPhone and iPad that challenges players to unscramble interlocking rings and reveal a hidden Instagram photograph. CircleGram players can then 'like' the photograph or follow its creator just as though they were using Instagram. Unaffiliated with Instagram but drawing on its API, CircleGram can generate puzzles from any of the hundreds of millions of photographs on Instagram.

CircleGram's Easy level puts the focus squarely on the right brain: find continuities in the rings and figure out how they align to form a photo. But go past Easy to Medium, Hard, or Expert and it's time for logic and reasoning: turn one ring and another may turn, requiring strategic thinking to get the circles in line. Fun and unexpected Game Center achievements as well as a global leaderboard make solving CircleGram puzzles all the more addictive. And as an added bonus, players can choose their own iTunes soundtrack to enjoy while solving puzzles or listen to CircleGram's built-in ambient track.

Out of the box, CircleGram offers puzzles based on photographs currently popular on Instagram or over 250 Instagrams built into the game. With a 99-cent upgrade, puzzles can be generated from a player's own Instagrams, their feed, or any hashtag they choose.

The iPhone and iPad-optimized versions of CircleGram can be downloaded for free here. Screenshots and more information are available at www.circlegram.com.
